Meet the Characters

Freddy Fazbear

The iconic bear and lead singer of the animatronics.

Bonnie the Bunny

The guitarist of the band, known for his hopping gait.

Chica the Chicken

The singer who loves pizza and has a habit of stalking players.

Foxy the Pirate

The elusive animatronic who hides away in Pirate Cove.

FNaF Locations

Freddy Fazbear's Pizza

The iconic pizzeria where the animatronics perform during the day and roam freely at night. Many mysterious events have taken place here, making it a central location in the FNaF universe.

Sister Location

The underground facility where advanced animatronics like Baby and Funtime Foxy reside. It's known for its dangerous secrets and advanced technology.

Fazbear's Fright

A horror attraction built to capitalize on the eerie history of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. It burns down at the end of FNaF 3, but the mysteries remain.

Fredbear's Family Diner

The original location that started it all. This diner is infamous for the bite incident that changed everything.

Animatronic Mechanics

Each animatronic has unique mechanics that change the way players must interact with them. Understanding their behavior is key to survival:

  • Freddy: Moves between the camera and the player's location, becoming more aggressive if the player fails to monitor him.
  • Bonnie: Primarily comes from the left side and can enter the room quickly if the player is not careful.
  • Chica: Approaches from the right and can be deterred by flashing the light.
  • Foxy: Stays in Pirate Cove until the player fails to check on him frequently, at which point he will sprint towards the player.

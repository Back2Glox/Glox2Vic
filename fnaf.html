Character Descriptions

Freddy Fazbear

The main mascot of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. He is a brown bear who loves to sing. Freddy is known for his friendliness, but he can become aggressive during the night!

Bonnie the Bunny

Bonnie is a lavender-colored rabbit who plays guitar. He is quite shy and prefers to stay in the shadows, but he can be a dangerous opponent when provoked.

Chica the Chicken

Chica is a yellow chicken who loves to eat pizza. She is usually seen holding a cupcake and is known for her loud and playful demeanor.

Foxy the Pirate

Foxy is a pirate fox who lives in Pirate Cove. He is known for his speed and agility, making him a formidable threat at night!
